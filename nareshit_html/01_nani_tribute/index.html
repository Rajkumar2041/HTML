Nani, whose real name is Ghanta Naveen Babu, is an Indian film actor and producer known for his work in the Telugu film industry. Born on February 24, 1984, in Hyderabad, India, Nani initially aspired to work in the film industry in a behind-the-scenes capacity, particularly as a director. However, his talents led him to the acting world, where he quickly gained recognition for his performances. Nani made his acting debut with the film Astha Chamma in 2008, which garnered him positive reviews and established him as a promising newcomer. Over the years, he has been part of several successful films, including Eega (2012), Bhale Bhale Magadivoy (2015), and Jersey (2019), among others. His versatility as an actor is evident in the wide range of roles he has portrayed, from romantic comedies to intense dramas. Nani's distinctive acting style, charm, and ability to connect with audiences have made him a popular figure in the industry. His contributions as a producer with his production house, Wall Poster Cinema, also reflect his passion for cinema. Nani's dedication to his craft and his talent have cemented his position as one of the leading actors in the Telugu film industry
